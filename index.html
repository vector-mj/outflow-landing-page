OutFLow is a platform which grants you the access to all humankind knowledge.
It portrays a graph of what has been learnt, what has been discovered, and what is yet to come.
No matter where you live, you can drop college and pick up OutFlow, Choose what you want to learn, your learning preferences, and your favourite teacher, it will create a model with all your personal preferences and through XR/AR you can see anything what could seem impossible to see.
You learn from OutFlow, you contribute to its knowledge content.
We share world's content, you share your brain with the world.
